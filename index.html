About us

Here at ‘THE BOOK ROOM’ we mix together the aspects of a book club and a tea room. We are based in London and we believe that reading is a habit that everyone can pick up.

Numerous studies have shown that reading at least 15/30 minutes a day has many benefits. This amount of time can even be divided into smaller chunks throughout the day.

However it’s not so important how long you read, but what you read. Reading a little bit everyday,even if it’s just a chapter or a page, will help you be consistent and will make reading part of your daily routine.
